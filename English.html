We’ve arrived in China, in the wonderful city of Beijing. The journey has been too late. We’re staying in a hotel near the city, it's very nice! The weather in the city is a bit strange, a moment ago it was very sunny, but then it got cloudy and it started to rain. We’ve been through the "Let's take care of the pandas!" project, in which we help to keep the pandas' habitat in good condition. We clean the habitat of the pandas, the river and cut the leaves from the trees, we classify the garbage that was collected; we gave them something to eat, well, to tell the truth, too much bamboo. We also checked that the health of all the pandas was fine. Tomorrow we’ll return to the pandas, we’ll feed them again, we’ll put places where they can play and have fun and we’ll bring more pandas to the habitat

Let's take care of the pandas!

Daniel Salazar Jaimes, Aug 28 2022
Read ...

I am volunteering to help preserve the red pandas in Beijing and it is very nice to work here, I have been cleaning the panda cages, I have also been playing with the pandas and I have been helping in the research of areas to leave. pandas in good living conditions. today is the second day that I work and it has gone very well, I met the owners of the panda clinic and it was very nice, they are in beijing and I stayed in a hotel near the center, I did a lot there, I have been living with animals sick or badly injured by scrapes and fractures, I have also been looking at how to treat them and have read a lot about veterinary medicine. The weather in Beijing was very warm and at first I decided to wear spring clothes to go to work to "live save the pandas". Without a doubt, it was very incredible to work, today on my last day is the best thing I have experienced this year. We plan to go next week to check out the fruits of our labor, visit the vet, and visit the nearby lake. see you later!!
